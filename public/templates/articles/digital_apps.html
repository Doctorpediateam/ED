Digital Apps

There are a number of digital apps you can use that will help you with erectile dysfunction issues. Some popular apps are listed below.

Erectile Dysfunction Self Test

WEBSITE: https://www.counseling-office.com

Price: $2.99/month

ITUNES:  N/A

GOOGLE PLAY:

Function: A four-part exam that tests for erectile dysfunction.

Background: Created by Viennese psychotherapist/sex therapist R.L Fellner.

General Comments: If the tests show you have erectile dysfunction, you can pay for an additional in-depth test.

What It Does Best: Gives you a better idea if you have erectile dysfunction.

Why It’s Famous: It’s free!

The Drawback: It costs money to advance from the initial four tests – at $19 per item.

Erectile Dysfunction Reverser

WEBSITE: N/A

Price: $2.99/month

ITUNES:N/A

GOOGLE PLAY: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.Erectile.Dysfunction.Reverser&hl=en

Function: Purports to teach how to reverse erectile dysfunction.

Background: It was updated as recently as April 3, 2017, and has over 1,000 installs.

General Comments: They claim to give step-by-step instructions to help you maintain erections.

What It Does Best: Gives you a rough idea if you have erectile dysfunction.

Why It’s Famous: Good ratings, and it’s free.

The Drawback: Curing erectile dysfunction simply isn’t this easy.

Erection, Erectile Dysfunction Prostate, Potency

WEBSITE: http://www.vqol.com/author/artur-litvinov

ITUNES: https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/erection-erectile-dysfunction-prostate-potency/id1098173953?mt=8

GOOGLE PLAY: N/A

Function: Provides a series of exercise videos aimed at helping with erectile dysfunction.

Background: Created by Artur Litvinov who has produced a series of digital medical apps.

General Comments: You can use it for exercise but don’t expect it to cure your erectile dysfunction.

What It Does Best: Gives personalized exercise routines and provides timers.

Why It’s Famous: People assume they’re going to cure their erectile dysfunction by using it, but nothing is guaranteed.

The Drawback: It’s a spin-off of the manufacturer’s other app GuyFit which costs $1.99

Sex Drive

WEBSITE: http://appsliced.co/apps/dev/jakub-koter

ITUNES: https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/sex-drive/id330161949?mt=8

GOOGLE PLAY: N/A

Function: It plays a binaural tone they claim promotes your sex life.

Background: The developer, Jakub Koter, claims that binaural tones can improve your health and has put together a series of apps around that idea.

General Comments: Run, don’t walk, in the opposite direction. Binaural tones aren’t scientifically proven to increase sex drive and the sounds are not exactly romantic.

What It Does Best: Makes you laugh.

Why It’s Famous: Famous for being not so great.

The Drawback: Not only is there a lack of evidence for effectiveness, these tones can kill the mood very quickly.

Erectile Dysfunction Mini Atlas

WEBSITE: http://www.pocket.md/2768/erectile-dysfunction-mini-atlas-for-iphone.html

ITUNES: https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/miniatlas-erectile-dysfunction/id427597651?mt=8

GOOGLE PLAY: N/A

Function: An app for Doctors to help quickly explain details about erectile dysfunction to patients with graphics.

Background: This app is backed by a big database of images.

General Comments: It’s a great app for doctors.

What It Does Best: Provides doctors images that they can easily share with patients; images that spell out details and answer questions.

Why It’s Famous: It’s good at what it does and is easy to use and cheap.

The Drawback: It’s not aimed at the average layman.
