Erectile Dysfunction Foundation

There are a variety of non-profit organizations you can turn to for help with erectile dysfunction solutions.

Website: http://www.givingmenhope.org

What They Do: Their mission is to teach about all things erectile dysfunction, as well as aid men in finding appropriate treatments.

How You Can Use Them: They offer man-to-man support and education to reach out and see what they can do for you.

Reliability: Just last year alone they reached over 30,000 men – and they grow every day.

Men's Health Network

Website: http://menshealthnetwork.org

What They Do: This national nonprofit works with both men and boys to advocate health awareness and prevention of disease.

How You Can Use Them: You can get involved by taking advantage of their screening programs and their educational tools, as well as advocacy opportunities.

Reliability: They’re a legit operation who have been around since 2000.

American Society for Reproductive Medicine

Website: http://www.auanet.org

What They Do: Their central focus is supporting funding and advocacy for young urology researchers.

How You Can Use Them: They create and develop education for urologists so they’re not likely to be able to offer more support than the information you can find on their site.

Reliability: Founded in 1902, it’s as reliable as you can get.

European Society For Sexual Medicine

Website: http://www.essm.org

What They Do: It’s a members-only based site dedicated to both male and female sexual dysfunction.

How You Can Use Them: You can find limited information that might be of help here. Perhaps you can steer your doctor here to see if they can learn anything new about ED.

Reliability: Members love it, but you have to be a scientist or a healthcare professional in the field of sexual medicine to sign up.

The Foundation For Male Studies

Website: http://www.malestudies.org

What They Do: Their primary focus is creating programs dedicated to male studies in both universities and charter schools.

How You Can Use Them: It’s another website aimed at professionals, but you might be able to glean some new information or contacts here [http://www.malestudies.org/library.html].

Reliability: They’re a legit operation who’ve been around for 15 years.
