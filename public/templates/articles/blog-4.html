The Six Tips to Talking With Your Partner About ED

This blog is mainly for the ladies (and some men) – specifically to give you some tips on how to talk to the man in your life suffering from erectile dysfunction.

Understanding

Know that it’s not necessarily you that’s the reason behind your man suffering from erectile dysfunction. In 80% of cases, there’s a physical reason behind the ED, ranging from blood pressure issues, diabetes, heart disease and more.

Pro-Active

If you wait for your man to discuss his erectile dysfunction issues you might be in for a very long wait! Find a way to be open and honest with him because communication is vital. Yes, there will be embarrassment on both sides, but the majority of it will lay with him, so you need to be able to be the proactive one.

The Blame Game

In short: don’t play it. Whatever has led to this point has happened and angrily pointing out reasons that got him to this place are not helpful, despite any truth to these reasons.

Timing Is Everything

Once you’re ready to talk, don’t choose a time that makes him feel cornered (during a meal, for example). Turn your phone off and make sure no visitors are coming over. Try some place neutral like going for a walk in the park. Words Are Everything Remember that whenever you do choose a time to speak about the issue might still cause him to be defensive – so be persistent but not pushy. Try using sentences beginning with “we” as opposed to “you” so he doesn’t feel like he’s being accused of anything. There aren’t any speeches out there so say what feels right while at the same time being aware that you don’t want him on the defensive.

Acceptance

If he’s not ready to talk about it, then you can only accept it and move on, waiting for a better time to arise. Tell him that you’ll talk to him about it another time though; changing the subject completely and quickly doesn’t achieve anything. If he does seem receptive to talking, keep it light and suggest he see a doctor if need be. Doctors mostly only need to take blood samples, so there’s nothing for him to be afraid of.
